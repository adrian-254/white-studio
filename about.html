About Us

Get to know the team behind eMotions

Our Story

eMotions was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate filmmakers who shared a love for capturing genuine human emotions and telling authentic stories. We saw that traditional wedding videos often failed to capture the true essence of such a significant life event.

Our mission was simple: to revolutionize wedding videography by bringing a cinematic, storytelling approach that focuses on emotions, moments, and the unique personality of each couple.

Since then, we've had the privilege of documenting hundreds of weddings, each one unique and beautiful in its own way. We've filmed intimate elopements with just two witnesses and grand celebrations with hundreds of guests. Through it all, our commitment to authentic storytelling has remained our guiding principle.

Our Approach

At eMotions, we believe that the best wedding films are created when we take the time to truly understand the couple. That's why we begin every project with a conversation about your story - how you met, what you love about each other, and what makes your relationship unique.

On your wedding day, we work discreetly in the background, capturing genuine moments as they unfold naturally. We don't stage or direct; instead, we observe and document, allowing you to fully enjoy your day without interruption.

After the wedding, our skilled editors craft a film that tells your story in a way that reflects your personalities and the atmosphere of your celebration. We pay close attention to pacing, music selection, and color grading to create a film that's as unique as your relationship.
